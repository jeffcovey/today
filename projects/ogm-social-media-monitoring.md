## Project Overview

Set up systematic monitoring of OlderGay.Men mentions across social media and the web to understand community reach, respond to discussions, and track brand awareness.

**Status:** Planning
**Priority:** Low (good delegation candidate)
**Estimated Setup Time:** 2-3 hours
**Ongoing Time:** 30 min/week (or delegated)

## Goals

- Track mentions of OlderGay.Men across platforms
- Identify community discussions and needs
- Respond to questions or concerns promptly
- Build awareness of site reach and impact
- Find partnership/collaboration opportunities

## Monitoring Workflow

### Weekly Tasks (30 minutes)
1. Check Google Alerts digest
2. Run saved searches on main platforms
3. Screenshot/save important mentions
4. Create brief summary report
5. Flag items needing response
6. Add positive mentions to testimonials

### Response Guidelines
- Thank positive mentions
- Address concerns/questions
- Correct misinformation
- Invite people to join community
- Build relationships with influencers

## Success Metrics

- Number of mentions per month
- Sentiment analysis (positive/negative/neutral)
- Response rate to questions
- New members from social mentions
- Partnership opportunities identified

## Resources Needed

- Google account for alerts
- Social media accounts for searching
- 30 min/week time commitment (or VA budget)
- Simple tracking spreadsheet
