# Six-Week Contact Reminders

> [!note] Requires [Obsidian Tasks](https://obsidian-tasks-group.github.io/obsidian-tasks/) plugin

Stay in touch with friends and family. Research suggests reaching out every 6 weeks helps maintain meaningful relationships.

## How to Use

1. Add a task for each person you want to stay in touch with
2. When you contact them, mark the task complete
3. The recurrence will create a new reminder in 6 weeks
